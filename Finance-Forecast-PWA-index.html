"Total going out" includes the full card bill — spent for others included — since that's the real amount that has to leave the account. "Left in hand from salary" only subtracts your own share, since the "spent for others" portion isn't really your expense even though it's on your card. Active card EMIs aren't added separately — they're already inside the linked card's outstanding above.
